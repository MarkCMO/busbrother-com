About BusBrother

Central Florida's trusted charter bus and group transportation company. Serving 120+ cities from Tampa to Fort Lauderdale.

Who We Are

BusBrother is a professional charter bus and group transportation company based on Florida's Space Coast. We specialize in connecting groups across Central and South Florida with reliable, comfortable motorcoach and minibus service for any occasion.

From cruise port shuttles at Port Canaveral to corporate event transportation in Orlando, from wedding guest shuttles on the Gulf Coast to school field trips to Kennedy Space Center - BusBrother handles it all with professionalism, local expertise, and 24/7 availability.

Our Mission

We believe group transportation should be simple, reliable, and stress-free. Our mission is to provide the highest quality charter bus service in Florida so your group can travel together safely and comfortably while we handle every detail.

Our Service Area

BusBrother serves the entire Central and South Florida corridor, from Tampa and St. Petersburg on the Gulf Coast, through Orlando and the Space Coast, up to Daytona Beach, and south to Fort Lauderdale and West Palm Beach. Our 120+ city coverage area includes every major airport, cruise port, theme park, convention center, and event venue in the region.
