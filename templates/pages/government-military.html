Government and Military Transportation Services

Government agencies and military installations have transportation needs that go beyond what a standard charter bus booking requires. Security, reliability, compliance, and accountability are not optional - they are baseline requirements. When service members, government employees, or official delegations need group transportation, the carrier must meet strict standards for driver screening, vehicle condition, insurance coverage, and operational compliance.

BusBrother serves government and military clients across Florida with the same rigorous carrier vetting we apply to all bookings, plus additional attention to the security and compliance requirements that government transportation demands. Every carrier in our network holds a valid USDOT number, active FMCSA operating authority, and a minimum of $5,000,000 in commercial auto liability insurance. All drivers hold valid CDL licenses with passenger endorsements and have passed criminal background checks, drug and alcohol testing, and motor vehicle record reviews.

Whether you need transportation for a unit movement, a government conference, base shuttle service, an official delegation visit, or emergency response personnel, BusBrother has the carrier network and logistics capability to support your mission.

Security and Compliance

BusBrother takes the security requirements of government and military transportation seriously. Here is how our carrier vetting process meets the standards expected by government clients:

  • ✓Criminal background checks on all drivers - Every driver in the BusBrother network has passed a criminal background screening including federal and state criminal history and sex offender registry checks
  • ✓Drug and alcohol testing program - All drivers participate in FMCSA-mandated drug and alcohol testing including pre-employment, random, post-accident, and reasonable suspicion testing
  • ✓Valid USDOT number and FMCSA operating authority - Every carrier is registered with the U.S. Department of Transportation and holds active federal operating authority for passenger transportation
  • ✓$5,000,000 minimum commercial auto liability insurance - Insurance certificates are verified before any carrier is assigned to a government trip
  • ✓Satisfactory FMCSA safety rating - No carriers with Conditional or Unsatisfactory safety ratings are used for any BusBrother trip
  • ✓Vehicle inspection compliance - All vehicles maintain current annual DOT inspections, and drivers perform pre-trip and post-trip inspections for every assignment
  • ✓Hours of service compliance - Drivers follow federal HOS regulations and use electronic logging devices to prevent fatigue-related incidents
  • ✓GPS tracking on all vehicles - Real-time vehicle tracking allows dispatch to monitor location and provide ETAs to your coordination team

Base access note: For transportation requiring access to military installations, please provide base access requirements and visitor procedures in your quote request. Drivers may need to undergo additional screening or obtain temporary visitor passes. Please allow extra lead time for trips requiring base access.

Frequently Asked Questions for Government Clients

Can drivers access military installations?

Base access procedures vary by installation. In most cases, the driver will need to be added to a visitor access list or obtain a temporary visitor pass. Please provide base access requirements in your quote request so we can coordinate with the carrier and driver in advance. Allow extra lead time for trips requiring base access.

Do you provide transportation for classified or sensitive missions?

BusBrother provides standard charter bus and shuttle service with background-checked drivers and DOT-compliant carriers. For transportation involving classified materials, sensitive personnel, or special security requirements beyond standard commercial charter service, please contact us to discuss your specific needs and we will advise on what we can accommodate.

Can you support large-scale movements with multiple buses?

Yes. BusBrother's carrier network spans all of Florida, and we regularly coordinate multi-vehicle movements for conferences, events, and group transportation. For military unit movements requiring multiple coaches, we can arrange synchronized departures, convoy routing, and coordinated arrival at the destination.

Do you offer recurring service for base shuttle routes?

Yes. BusBrother can set up recurring daily, weekly, or monthly shuttle service for base facilities, government campuses, and inter-facility routes. Recurring contracts include consistent driver assignment (when possible), fixed scheduling, and discounted rates compared to one-time bookings.
