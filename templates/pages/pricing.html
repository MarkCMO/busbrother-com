What Affects Charter Bus Pricing?

  • ✓Distance and Duration - Longer trips cost more. Short local trips are billed hourly (5-hour minimum). Long-distance trips are billed per mile.
  • ✓Group Size - Larger buses cost more total but less per person. A motorcoach for 50 people costs about $14-$25 per person for a local trip.
  • ✓Season - Peak cruise season (November-April) and spring break cost more. January, February, and July are typically cheapest.
  • ✓Day of Week - Weekday trips are typically 10-20% cheaper than weekends.
  • ✓Trip Type - One-way trips cost less than round trips. Multi-stop itineraries are priced based on total time and distance.
  • ✓Vehicle Type - Premium coaches with leather seats cost more than standard motorcoaches.

Frequently Asked Questions About Pricing

Charter bus rental in Florida typically costs $150 to $300 per hour for a full-size motorcoach (45-57 passengers), $100 to $200 per hour for a minibus (15-30 passengers), and $125 to $250 per hour for a premium coach (30-40 passengers). Per-mile rates range from $4 to $8 per mile. The total cost depends on distance, duration, group size, and time of year.
A full-day charter bus rental in Florida typically ranges from $1,200 to $2,500 for a motorcoach, $800 to $1,500 for a minibus, and $1,000 to $2,000 for a premium coach. Multi-day rentals often include discounted daily rates.
A private group shuttle from Orlando to Port Canaveral typically costs $400 to $800 for a minibus or $700 to $1,200 for a full motorcoach. Per-person pricing on a shared shuttle ranges from $25 to $45 per person.
Charter bus is the cheapest per-person option for groups of 10 or more. A group of 30 taking a charter bus from Orlando to MCO costs roughly $15 to $25 per person versus $30 to $50 per person for rideshare.
Book at least 2 weeks in advance for standard trips and 3 to 4 weeks for peak season. For weddings and corporate events, book 1 to 2 months ahead for the best vehicle selection and pricing.
Most charter bus rentals have a 4 to 5 hour minimum for local trips. Long-distance one-way transfers do not have an hourly minimum - they are priced by distance.
Tipping is not required but is appreciated. A typical tip for a charter bus driver is 10 to 15 percent of the total rental cost, or $5 to $10 per person for excellent service.
