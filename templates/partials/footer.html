BusBrother is a Florida lead-matching and referral service operated by WETYR Corporation in Cape Canaveral, FL. We connect groups with independent, licensed FDOT/FMCSA motor carriers. BusBrother is not a transportation broker (49 USC 13102), not a motor carrier, and does not operate any vehicles. We do not hold customer payment or set binding transportation prices — the carrier you book with quotes, bills, and provides the service directly. All carriers in our network maintain valid USDOT registration, FMCSA operating authority, $5M+ commercial auto liability insurance, and CDL drivers with passenger endorsements.
