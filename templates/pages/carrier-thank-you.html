Application Received

Thank you for applying to join the BusBrother carrier network. We'll review your credentials and respond within 2 business days.

What happens next

  1. Credential verification (1-2 business days) — We check your USDOT number, FMCSA safety rating, insurance coverage, and DOT vehicle inspection status against the FMCSA SAFER system at safer.fmcsa.dot.gov.
  2. Approval decision — You'll receive an approval email once your credentials are verified. If we need additional information, we'll email or call you.
  3. Lead delivery begins — Once approved, you'll start receiving lead emails for trips matching your service area. Each email includes full customer contact details and trip requirements.
  4. You quote, book, and bill the customer directly — BusBrother stays out of the transaction once we forward the lead.

Have additional materials?

If you'd like to send us your current insurance certificate (ACORD 25), recent DOT inspection reports, fleet photos, or carrier references, reply to your application confirmation email or send to info@busbrother.com.

Questions?

Email us at info@busbrother.com with any questions about the network, lead routing, or our verification process. We respond within 4 hours during business hours.
