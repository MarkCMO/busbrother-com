Florida Passenger-Transport Acquisition Targets

Charter motorcoach, tour, trolley, shuttle and school-bus operators headquartered in Florida, compiled from association directories (FMA, UMA), the FMCSA carrier registry, the PortMiami licensed-operator directory, state tourism listings, and operator websites. National brokers and lead-gen sites that don't own fleets were excluded. Every phone and email was pulled from a live source, none were invented.

Data honesty: owner/executive names are only shown where publicly listed (roughly a third of rows). Rows marked VERIFY or ENRICH, and the PortMiami-licensed shuttle operators (phone only), need a quick check before outreach. Sunbiz officer records (search.sunbiz.org) are the fastest way to fill missing owner names. Direct personal CEO emails are rarely public; use the company email plus the named contact to route.
