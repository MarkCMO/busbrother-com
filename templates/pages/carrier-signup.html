Join the BusBrother Carrier Network

Florida-licensed charter bus operators: get matched with qualified group transportation leads across all 17 Brevard County cities and 120+ Florida cities. Currently free to apply.

Phase 1: Free Lead Access

BusBrother is currently in our launch phase. Every lead we generate is delivered free of charge to approved carriers in our network.

Founding Carrier program: The first 5 carriers approved to our network receive lifetime free lead access as Founding Carriers — no per-lead fees, no commission, no monthly subscription, ever. Apply now to claim a Founding Carrier slot.

What we send carriers

  • ✓Customer trip details: date, time, route, group size, service type, special requirements
  • ✓Customer contact info (name, email, phone) so you can quote and book directly
  • ✓Trip notes including ADA requirements, multi-stop, luggage assistance

What we require

  • ✓Valid USDOT number with active FMCSA operating authority
  • ✓FMCSA safety rating of Satisfactory or None (not Conditional or Unsatisfactory)
  • ✓Minimum $5,000,000 in commercial auto liability insurance
  • ✓Current DOT vehicle inspections on every motorcoach
  • ✓CDL drivers with Passenger endorsement (CDL-P) and clean background checks
  • ✓Response within 2 hours of receiving a lead during business hours

How it works

  1. Submit the application below — we verify your credentials within 2 business days
  2. Once approved, you start receiving lead emails for trips that match your service area
  3. You contact the customer directly with your quote — book and bill them directly
  4. BusBrother stays out of the transaction; we don't take a cut on Phase 1 leads

Note: BusBrother is a lead-matching and referral service, not a transportation broker or motor carrier. All transportation is provided by independent, licensed FDOT/FMCSA carriers like you. We do not hold customer payment or set binding prices.
