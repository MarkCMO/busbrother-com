How BusBrother works for carriers

BusBrother is a Florida-focused lead-matching service connecting groups with licensed charter bus and motorcoach operators. Customers submit trip requests; we verify the request, match it to carriers in our network whose service area and fleet fit the trip, and forward the lead to those carriers. Carriers then quote and book the customer directly.

We are not a transportation broker, not a motor carrier, and not a fleet operator. We do not hold customer payment, do not set binding prices, and do not intermediate the carrier-customer relationship once we forward the lead.

Phase 1: Free leads (currently active)

Every lead BusBrother generates is delivered free of charge to approved carriers in our network. There are no per-lead fees, no commission on bookings, and no monthly subscription during Phase 1.

Founding Carrier program
The first 5 carriers approved to our network are designated Founding Carriers and receive lifetime free lead access. After we reach 5 Founding Carriers, new carriers joining the network will participate in our Phase 2 pricing model (below).

Phase 2: Per-lead access fee (coming later)

Once we reach our Founding Carrier cap, new carriers will pay a per-lead access fee to receive customer contact details. We will publish exact pricing before Phase 2 begins. Founding Carriers will continue to receive every lead at no cost.

Phase 3: Bid auction (future)

Eventually, lead access will move to a competitive bid auction: carriers will bid on lead access amounts, and the top bidders will win the customer contact. This phase will only launch once we have sufficient carrier supply for healthy bidding dynamics. Founding Carriers will receive guaranteed priority access regardless of auction outcomes.

What we send you

  • ✓Customer trip details: date, time, pickup, drop-off, passenger count, service type
  • ✓Customer contact info: name, email, phone — so you can quote and book directly
  • ✓Special requirements: ADA wheelchair access, multi-stop routing, luggage assistance, return trip needs
  • ✓Trip notes and any extra information the customer provided

What we require

  • ✓Valid USDOT number with active FMCSA operating authority (MC number)
  • ✓FMCSA safety rating of Satisfactory or None (not Conditional or Unsatisfactory)
  • ✓Minimum $5,000,000 in commercial auto liability insurance, current and on file
  • ✓Current annual DOT vehicle inspections on every motorcoach in the fleet
  • ✓CDL drivers with Passenger endorsement (CDL-P) and clean background checks
  • ✓FMCSA-compliant drug & alcohol testing program (49 CFR Part 382)
  • ✓Response to lead within 2 hours during business hours, 4 hours after hours
  • ✓Honest, transparent pricing — no upsells once the trip is booked
