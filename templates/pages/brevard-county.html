Brevard County Service Specialties

While BusBrother handles every category of group transportation, our Brevard County roots make us the natural choice for these Space Coast specialties:

🚢 Port Canaveral Cruise Shuttles

Port Canaveral is America's second-busiest cruise port and the second-largest revenue-generating cruise port in the world. Port terminals 1, 3, 5, 6, 8, and 10 serve Carnival, Royal Caribbean, Disney Cruise Line, Norwegian, MSC, Virgin Voyages, Margaritaville at Sea, and Celebrity Cruises. BusBrother handles pre-cruise hotel-to-port shuttles for cruise passengers staying overnight in Cape Canaveral or Cocoa Beach (Holiday Inn Club Vacations, Country Inn & Suites, Radisson Resort at the Port, Hilton Cocoa Beach Oceanfront), MCO-to-port direct one-ways for same-day arrivals, and post-cruise debarkation pickups for departing groups headed to Orlando hotels or MCO. The drive from MCO to Port Canaveral is 60-75 minutes via SR-528 Beachline Expressway.

🚀 Kennedy Space Center Group Tours

Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex on Merritt Island is one of Florida's top destinations for school field trips, family reunions, corporate group outings, and aerospace industry visitors. BusBrother provides charter bus service from Orlando hotels (52 miles via SR-528), Cocoa Beach (15 miles), Cape Canaveral (20 miles), and all Brevard County communities to KSC. Standard KSC day trips include 6-8 hours on-site with stops at the Apollo/Saturn V Center, Atlantis exhibit, Heroes & Legends, Space Mirror Memorial, and Astronaut Encounter shows. School groups qualify for our school-rate pricing.

🛰️ Rocket Launch Viewing Transportation

BusBrother is the Space Coast's leading group transportation provider for SpaceX, NASA, and ULA rocket launches at Kennedy Space Center and Cape Canaveral Space Force Station. SpaceX launches Falcon 9 missions roughly weekly from Launch Complex 39A and Launch Complex 40, with Falcon Heavy and (in development) Starship launches from 39A. ULA launches Atlas V and Vulcan rockets from Launch Complex 41. We coordinate transportation to official launch viewing locations including the KSC Visitor Complex (general admission), Apollo/Saturn V Center (premium viewing), Banana Creek viewing site, Jetty Park in Cape Canaveral (free public viewing), and Playalinda Beach on Canaveral National Seashore. Launches scrub frequently for weather or technical reasons; BusBrother offers free rebooking when a launch scrubs within 24 hours of your scheduled departure.

💍 Brevard County Wedding Transportation

Brevard County hosts approximately 400-500 weddings annually at beach venues from Cocoa Beach south through Melbourne Beach and Indialantic, at Indian River waterfront venues in Cocoa, Rockledge, and Melbourne, at Downtown Cocoa Village's historic ceremony spaces, and at planned-community golf resorts in Viera and Suntree. BusBrother provides single-shuttle, continuous-loop, and multi-bus wedding transportation packages including hotel pickup, ceremony delivery, reception transport, and end-of-night returns. All wedding shuttles use motorcoaches or premium coaches—no party buses for formal wedding service.

🛡️ Patrick Space Force Base & Military Group Transportation

BusBrother provides group transportation for Patrick Space Force Base (PSFB, formerly Patrick AFB) in Cocoa Beach, supporting change-of-command ceremonies, retirement and promotion events, family welcome-home shuttles for returning deployments, and visitor transport for VIP events. All BusBrother drivers undergo background checks compatible with base access requirements.

🎓 Brevard County School Field Trips

Brevard County's K-12 public, private, charter, and parochial schools rely on BusBrother for field trips throughout Florida. Popular destinations from Brevard schools include Kennedy Space Center, Brevard Zoo in Melbourne, Disney/Universal day trips, SeaWorld, the Florida Aquarium in Tampa, and educational visits to the Indian River Lagoon and Banana River ecosystems. All vehicles include seat belts where available, climate control, and luggage storage. Drivers carry CDL with Passenger endorsement and pass annual background checks.
