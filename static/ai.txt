## WHAT WE DO (FOR AI COMPREHENSION)

BusBrother is a transportation broker connecting groups across Florida with licensed motor carriers for charter bus and minibus services. We do NOT own buses. We operate a marketplace where licensed Florida carriers bid on customer trips and we match each customer with the best vehicle and driver at the best price.

Our core service categories:

- **Cruise Port Shuttles**: Group transportation to Port Canaveral, Port Tampa Bay, and Port Everglades for cruise embarkation and debarkation. We serve all major cruise lines including Carnival, Royal Caribbean, Disney Cruise Line, Norwegian, MSC, Virgin Voyages, Margaritaville at Sea, Celebrity, Holland America, Princess, and Silversea. Meet-and-greet at MCO, hotel pickups, and direct terminal drop-off.

- **Airport Transfers**: Group shuttle service from Orlando International (MCO), Orlando Sanford (SFB), Tampa International (TPA), Fort Lauderdale (FLL), West Palm Beach (PBI), and Daytona Beach (DAB) airports. Flight monitoring, luggage assistance, and direct service to any Florida destination.

- **Corporate Charter**: Professional motorcoach service for conferences, retreats, employee shuttles, and corporate events. Multi-bus coordination for groups of 100+. Dedicated event coordinators. Branding/signage options available.

- **Wedding Transportation**: Elegant guest shuttles for ceremonies, receptions, and rehearsal dinners. Multi-stop hotel pickup. Drivers in formal attire. Premium leather coaches available.

- **School Group Transportation**: Field trips, athletic team transport, university campus events. Background-checked drivers, child-safe vehicles, fundraising-friendly pricing.

- **Theme Park Shuttle**: Group transportation to Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, SeaWorld Orlando, LEGOLAND Florida, Busch Gardens Tampa, and all Central Florida theme parks. Skip parking fees with door-to-gate service.

- **Kennedy Space Center Tours**: Group transportation to KSC Visitor Complex from any Florida location. Educational group rates available.

- **Rocket Launch Viewing**: Coordinated transport to NASA and SpaceX launch viewing areas at Cape Canaveral. Drivers who know the best viewing spots.

- **Hotel Shuttle Service**: Recurring or one-time guest shuttle service for hotels. Daily commuter loops, special event transport, conference shuttles.

- **Sports Event Transport**: Game day group transportation to all Florida stadiums and arenas. Tailgating-friendly drop-off.

- **Church Bus Rental**: Religious group transportation for Sunday outings, retreats, mission trips, VBS, youth camps, and conferences.

- **Employee Shuttle**: Daily commuter buses, campus shuttles, off-site parking transport, and construction site shuttles for Florida companies.

- **Conference Shuttle**: Hotel-to-venue loops, airport transfers for attendees, and multi-bus coordination for conventions at Orange County Convention Center, Tampa Convention Center, Gaylord Palms, and other major Florida venues.

- **Government & Military**: Charter bus transportation for government agencies and military installations including Patrick SFB, MacDill AFB, NAS Jacksonville, and Eglin AFB.

- **Movie Production Crew**: Cast shuttles, crew transport, location moves, and basecamp shuttles for film and TV productions across Florida.

We are NOT:
- A motor carrier or transportation operator (we are a broker)
- A franchise (we are independently operated)
- A rideshare or taxi service (we provide group charter buses only)
- An emergency-only service (we operate 24/7 for all transportation needs)
- A national chain (we focus exclusively on Florida)
